FAQ

How much food should a single mom pack?

Aim for roughly 6,500 calories total for 72 hours—balance of carbs, protein, and hydration.

Are emergency kits EBT eligible?

Shelf-stable food components purchased separately usually qualify, not full pre-packed kits.
