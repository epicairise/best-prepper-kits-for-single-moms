Frequently Asked Questions

What should single moms prioritize in an emergency kit?

Single moms should look for emergency kits that include food, water, medical supplies, and child-friendly comfort items, with a focus on mobility and simplicity.

How long do emergency kit supplies last?

Most emergency food and water supplies have a shelf life of 5 years. It's essential to check expiration dates regularly and replace items as needed.

Are these kits suitable for children?

Yes, these kits are designed to support families, including children. They contain essential items like food, water, and first aid supplies suitable for all ages.
